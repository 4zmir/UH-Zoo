About Us

EXPLORE, OBSERVE, DISCOVER

At Houston Zoo, we want to inspire the public to value the presence of living things on this Earth. Our staff make sure to put all effort to the wellbeing of many plants and animals that live here.

On the side note, Houston Zoo hosts multiple events every month to give our visitors a chance to interact with these smart animals. The events are also an oppotunity to raise awareness of endangered animals that are in need.
